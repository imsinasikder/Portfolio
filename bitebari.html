A Brand of Sikder Agency

Introduction

BiteBari is a homemade food and dessert brand operating under the creative and entrepreneurial vision of Sikder Agency. More than just a cake business, BiteBari represents emotion, creativity, care, and the dream of building meaningful experiences through food.

The brand was created with the belief that every celebration, every gathering, and every special moment deserves something handmade, heartfelt, and memorable.

The name “BiteBari” reflects warmth, comfort, and sweetness. “Bite” symbolizes taste and enjoyment, while “Bari” represents home — together creating the idea of homemade happiness in every bite.

"Sweet Story in Every Bite."

What is BiteBari?

BiteBari is a homemade bakery and dessert brand focused on creating high-quality cakes, pastries, cupcakes, jar cakes, desserts, and future homemade food items with love, care, and creativity.

We are not just preparing food — we are building experiences. Every product is handcrafted with attention to detail, fresh ingredients, balanced flavor, and emotional value.

  • Homemade quality & Fresh preparation
  • Creative presentation & Customization
  • Customer satisfaction & Emotional connection
  • Affordable premium experiences

Our Specializations

Currently, BiteBari specializes in homemade baked and dessert products such as custom birthday cakes, chocolate cakes, vanilla cakes, cupcakes, and jar cakes.

In the future, we plan to expand into a larger homemade food ecosystem, including snacks, premium sweets, and nationwide delivery.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to create fresh, high-quality products that bring joy and comfort to people’s lives. We aim to become one of the most trusted homemade food brands in Bangladesh.

We aim to represent:

  • Quality & Creativity
  • Warmth & Reliability
  • Handmade excellence
  • Emotional storytelling through food

Why We Are Different

We focus on Homemade Quality, maintaining freshness and flavor in every product. Our Emotional Branding connects people to our desserts, and every order tells a story.

Achievements

We have successfully handled large-scale event orders, including a highlight of 300 dessert items delivered on time and sold out instantly.
